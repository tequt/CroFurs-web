    Welcome To CroFurs

    About us

    Welcome to the Crofurs site. We are a group of furries mostly from Croatia and around. If you are here, chances are that you like/want getting to know other members of the community either in person or simply online.

    All furries are welcome, no matter where you live.

    Where can you find us?

    We are active on many social networks, but most active are groups on telegram and discord. You can find links to those and other social platforms down below. We do small meet ups in bars (beer party :p) and sometimes organise a barbecue in the open.
